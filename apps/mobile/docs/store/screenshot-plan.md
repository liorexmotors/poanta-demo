# Poenta Store Screenshot Plan

## Goal
First screenshots must explain the product in Hebrew within 2–3 seconds: a clean Hebrew news feed that shows the point of each story and the source.

## Required sets

### Apple App Store
- iPhone 6.7 inch screenshots.
- iPhone 6.5 inch screenshots if required by current App Store Connect flow.
- iPad only if we keep `supportsTablet: true`; otherwise consider disabling tablet support before first submission to reduce screenshot/review scope.

### Google Play
- Android phone screenshots.
- Feature graphic 1024×500.

## Recommended first 5 screenshots — Hebrew copy

1. **הפואנטה של החדשות — במקום עוד גלילה**
   - Show main feed with strong cards and images.

2. **כותרת, הקשר ומקור — בכרטיס אחד**
   - Zoom on one card with source area visible.

3. **פיד עברי נקי שמתעדכן ממקורות ציבוריים**
   - Show multiple cards and freshness/time labels.

4. **מבינים מהר מה חשוב עכשיו**
   - Show top/latest section or breaking-style feed when implemented in native app.

5. **בלי הרשמה. בלי רעש. פשוט לקרוא.**
   - Show app opening state / clean feed.

## Visual direction
- Dark premium background.
- Yellow Poenta accent.
- Large readable Hebrew headlines.
- Real feed items, but avoid graphic/overly sensitive news in screenshots.
- Do not show internal dashboards, QA screens, logs, or operator views.
- Do not show “MVP”, “שלד”, “demo”, “test”, “mockup”, or implementation wording.

## Screenshot QA gates
- RTL text direction correct.
- No clipped Hebrew labels.
- No private/internal URLs.
- No duplicate same-story cards in visible frame.
- Source attribution visible but not dominant.
- Status bar/time/battery clean.
